How Dental Dash Pro Works

1. Patients Submit Treatment Requests

Patients submit their treatment details, findings letters, and X-rays to receive professional estimates.

2. You Provide Professional Estimates

Participating dental offices review patient requests and submit professional treatment estimates and recommendations.

3. Patients Schedule Consultations

Patients review treatment options and request in-person consultations with the office that fits their needs.
