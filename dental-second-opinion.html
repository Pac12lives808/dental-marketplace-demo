What is a dental second opinion?

A dental second opinion is an independent professional review of a recommended treatment plan by a separate licensed dentist. It helps patients in Phoenix and the East Valley confirm whether a proposed procedure is appropriate, understand alternative approaches, and feel confident before committing to care.

When should I get a second opinion on dental work?

Consider a second opinion before major procedures such as crowns, root canals, extractions, implants, or full-mouth restorations. If you are uncertain about a diagnosis, a recommended treatment, or the estimated cost, a professional second opinion from a licensed dentist can provide the clarity you need before moving forward.
