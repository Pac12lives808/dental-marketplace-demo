Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dental Dash Pro?

Dental Dash Pro is a platform that connects patients with licensed dental offices in Phoenix and the East Valley. Patients submit their treatment needs and receive professional treatment estimates and second opinions from verified providers.

How does it work for patients?

You describe your dental concern and submit a request. Licensed dental offices review your case and respond with professional treatment estimates. You compare approaches and costs before choosing a provider.

Are the dental offices licensed and verified?

Yes. All participating offices are licensed professionals. Dental Dash Pro verifies credentials before approving any office to participate in the platform.

Does Dental Dash Pro provide dental care directly?

No. Dental Dash Pro is a technology platform, not a dental office. We facilitate professional communication between patients and licensed dental providers.

Are treatment estimates informational only?

Yes. All treatment estimates are informational and must be confirmed during an in-office visit. Dental Dash Pro does not provide medical advice.

Is it worth getting a second opinion on dental work?

Yes. Dental treatment costs for the same procedure can vary significantly between offices in Phoenix and the East Valley. Dental Dash Pro makes it easy to compare multiple licensed offices at once so you can understand your options before committing to treatment.

How much does a dental crown cost?

Dental crown costs typically range from $950 to $1,400 or more depending on materials such as porcelain or zirconia, location, and the technology used by the office. Dental Dash Pro allows you to compare real treatment estimates from licensed dental offices in Phoenix and the East Valley before making a decision.

What is a dental treatment estimate?

A dental treatment estimate is a clinical breakdown of recommended procedures, materials, and expected costs provided by a licensed dentist. Dental Dash Pro collects multiple treatment estimates so patients can compare professional approaches and make informed decisions.
