Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth getting a second opinion on dental work?

Yes. Dental treatment costs for the same procedure can vary significantly between offices. Dental Dash Pro makes it easy to compare multiple licensed offices at once so you can understand your options before committing to treatment.

How much does a dental crown cost?

Dental crown costs typically range from $950 to $1,400 or more depending on materials such as porcelain or zirconia, location, and the technology used by the office. Dental Dash Pro allows you to compare real treatment estimates from licensed dental offices before making a decision.

What is a dental treatment estimate?

A dental treatment estimate is a clinical breakdown of recommended procedures, materials, and expected costs provided by a licensed dentist. Dental Dash Pro collects multiple treatment estimates so patients can compare professional approaches and make informed decisions.
