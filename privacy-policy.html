Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

Dental Dash Pro collects information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Patient information: Name, contact details, dental treatment needs, and any information you submit when requesting treatment estimates.
  • Dental office information: Practice name, contact information, professional credentials, and application details submitted by dental offices.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, and interactions with our platform collected automatically through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate connections between patients seeking dental estimates and participating dental offices.
  • Send treatment estimates and related communications.
  • Process dental office applications and manage provider relationships.
  • Improve our platform and develop new features.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the rights of users.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With dental offices: Patient treatment request details are shared with participating dental offices so they can provide estimates. Only relevant clinical and contact information needed to respond to your request is shared.
  • With patients: Dental office names, credentials, and estimate details are shared with patients who submitted the corresponding treatment request.
  • Service providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers (such as cloud hosting and analytics) who assist us in operating our platform.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

4. HIPAA and Health Information

Dental Dash Pro is a marketplace platform that facilitates the exchange of general dental treatment estimate information. We do not store Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA. Participating dental offices are independently responsible for their own HIPAA compliance. Treatment estimates provided through our platform are informational in nature and do not constitute a clinical diagnosis or treatment record.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. All data transmitted through our platform is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our platform. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data using cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. We do not use cookies for advertising purposes.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us at support@dentaldashpro.com. We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any legal obligations to retain certain records.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Opt out of certain data uses.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@dentaldashpro.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised date. Your continued use of our platform after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
