Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use Dental Dash Pro, we may collect information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, zip code, and dental treatment details you submit for review. We also collect standard usage data through Google Analytics, including pages visited, browser type, and general location data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to connect you with licensed dental offices that can review your treatment details and provide estimates or second opinions. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may use aggregate, anonymized data to improve our platform and services.

Sharing Your Information

Your submitted treatment details are shared with licensed dental office partners on the Dental Dash Pro platform for the purpose of providing estimates and clinical input. By submitting a case, you consent to this sharing. We do not share your personal contact information with dental offices without your explicit consent.

Cookies and Analytics

Dental Dash Pro uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. Google Analytics may place cookies on your device. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by visiting https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you submit through our platform. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Children's Privacy

Dental Dash Pro is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
